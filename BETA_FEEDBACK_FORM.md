# Beta Feedback Form

Use this after each 30 to 45 minute beta session.

## Tester

- Tester name:
- Date:
- Device/browser:
- Seller intent used:

## Search

- What did you search?
- Did the results match the location?
- Did the results match the category?
- Which result looked clearly wrong?
- Which result looked like the best lead?

## Lead Understanding

- Did you understand the lead card?
- Did Proof & Confidence help?
- Did you understand why a lead was recommended?
- What information was missing before you would call?
- Which lead would you call first, and why?

## Queues

- Did `Ring nå` make sense?
- Did `Må verifiseres` make sense?
- Did `Oppfølging` make sense?
- Did you trust the next action shown on the lead?
- Did you move any leads manually? If yes, why?

## Workflow

- Could you log an outcome without help?
- Could you set a follow-up without help?
- Did the follow-up appear where you expected?
- Did notes feel easy enough to use?
- Did export or copy behavior give you what you needed?

## Trust

- Did you trust the phone/company/location data?
- What made you uncertain?
- What proof would make you more comfortable calling?
- Did Hosted Verify & Enrich feel clear as a lightweight refresh?

## Overall

- What felt confusing?
- What felt useful?
- Would you use this tomorrow?
- What would make this 10x more useful?
- What should be removed or hidden?
