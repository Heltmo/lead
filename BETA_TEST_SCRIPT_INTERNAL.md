## Exact Test Path

1. Open the beta URL.
2. Search rørlegger i Sarpsborg.
3. Select seller intent in Hva selger du?.
4. Find 3 leads that look worth reviewing.
5. Move one lead to Ring nå.
6. Move one lead to Må verifiseres.
7. Log No answer on one lead.
8. Set follow-up for tomorrow.
9. Mark one lead Ikke relevant.
10. Refresh the page.
11. Confirm state is still correct.
12. Export or copy data.
13. Fill BETA_FEEDBACK_FORM.md.

## Stop Conditions

Stop the session and write down the issue if:

- beta access fails
- search fails without a clear error
- notes or follow-ups do not save
- the tester cannot understand what to do next
- the tester thinks the product sends email, places calls, syncs CRM, or writes outreach
- export fails after workflow state was created

## After Session

Sort feedback into:

- Did not understand
- Did not trust data
- Missing workflow
- Bug or broken state
