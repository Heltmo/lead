# Call Desk Polish V1

## Objective

Make Lead Machine feel like a focused daily sales desk instead of a dense lead/enrichment viewer.

The seller should be able to open the app and immediately know:

- who to call next
- whether the lead is ready, needs verification, should be followed up, or should be skipped
- what was last logged
- what recent search can be rerun

## Build Scope

- Make call readiness a first-class UI signal.
- Use call readiness in the queue and lead cards.
- Improve the selected lead detail with a compact call focus strip.
- Show more useful activity history instead of only a tiny latest log.
- Improve recent saved searches with lead count context and one-click rerun.

## Boundaries

Do not add Proff, SSB, outreach automation, email sending, telephony, auth, billing, CRM sync, or sales scripts.

## Success Criteria

- Lead cards and selected lead header show call readiness.
- Current call queue prioritizes call readiness.
- Activity history shows more than three entries when available.
- Saved searches show useful context and can be rerun directly.
- Existing readiness, OSINT, seller-fit, lead-machine and demo verifiers pass.
